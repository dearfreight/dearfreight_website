    • About Dear Freight

    At dearfreight.com, we specialize in seamless import and export solutions, connecting businesses in Pakistan with trusted global suppliers—especially from China. With a focus on reliability, transparency, and customer satisfaction, we simplify your freight journey from origin to destination.

    Vision

    To become Pakistan’s most trusted import-export partner, empowering businesses with efficient, reliable, and cost-effective global trade solutions.

    Mission

    Our mission is to simplify global trade for Pakistani businesses by providing dependable import-export services, with a focus on China-to-Pakistan freight, customs clearance, and end-to-end logistics support.
